Copyright and Plagiarism Requirements

This page describes LifeSync's expectations for copyright, plagiarism, original content, and intellectual property rights.

Last updated: May 25, 2026

LifeSync content

Unless otherwise stated, the LifeSync name, website text, app downloads, interface elements, and other original materials are owned by LifeSync or its creator. All rights are reserved except where a separate license says otherwise.

User-created content

LifeSync lets users create tasks, reminders, whiteboard drawings, whiteboard text, mind map nodes, notes, and related planning content. Users are responsible for ensuring that content they enter into LifeSync does not infringe another person's copyright, trademark, privacy, or other rights.

Plagiarism and third-party works

  • Do not copy, republish, or redistribute LifeSync materials without permission or a valid legal basis.
  • Do not remove notices, author names, license terms, or attribution information from third-party materials.
  • Do not submit or publish content as your own if it was created by another person or organization.
  • Get permission or confirm a valid exception before using copyrighted images, icons, text, audio, video, or code.
  • Do not upload or sync third-party content into whiteboards, mind maps, tasks, or notes unless you have the right to do so.

Fair use and exceptions

Some uses of copyrighted material may be permitted by fair use, public domain status, open licenses, or other exceptions. These determinations depend on facts and jurisdiction, so permission is recommended when the legal basis is unclear.
