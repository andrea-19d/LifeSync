Email

Anti-Spam Laws

This page explains LifeSync's current email practices and anti-spam expectations under laws such as CAN-SPAM and similar email marketing rules.

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Current email status

The LifeSync website does not currently offer a newsletter signup, marketing email list, or purchase checkout email flow. The app does use Firebase Authentication with email/password login and Google Sign-In, so account-related emails may be sent by authentication providers.

If email is added

  • Do not use false or misleading header information.
  • Do not use deceptive subject lines.
  • Identify commercial messages where required.
  • Include a valid sender contact address when required.
  • Provide a clear unsubscribe method and honor opt-out requests promptly.
  • Monitor email vendors and affiliates acting on LifeSync's behalf.

Transactional messages

Authentication, password reset, account verification, security, reminder, and service messages should be limited to their service purpose unless the user has separately consented to marketing or the law otherwise allows it.
